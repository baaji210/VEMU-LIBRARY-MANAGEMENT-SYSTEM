About Us

VEMU Institute Library

The VEMU Institute of Technology Library is a state-of-the-art academic resource centre serving thousands of students and faculty. Our digital management system enables seamless borrowing, real-time availability, and transparent fine management.

Our collection spans Engineering, Sciences, Mathematics, Humanities and Management — continuously updated with the latest academic publications.

🎯 Our Mission

Equitable access to knowledge that empowers students and faculty to achieve academic excellence and contribute to research and innovation.

📖 Our Collection

12,400+ books with 4 role-based access levels — Student, Faculty, Librarian, and Admin — each with tailored services and permissions.

🌐 Digital Services

Online catalogue, digital borrowing, automated fine calculation, member activity reports, book recommendations, and multi-role access.

🏆 Accreditations

NAAC A+ | NBA | UGC | JNTUA | AICTE — among the top autonomous institutions in Andhra Pradesh with over 20 years of excellence.
